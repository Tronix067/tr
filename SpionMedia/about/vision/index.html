Vision

Center Holding the Trend Scale

Acting as a scale in the system, strategically determining the trends and people we promote; operating with our own agents in a strong and centralized structure. Here, hierarchy is not imposed, superiority proven by results is essential. No one dominates another; the superior is seen through the results achieved.

What We Want?
  • Select trends and determine their direction.
  • Leave a cultural imprint through multi-disciplinary production (video, sound, 3D, gaming).
  • Turn the outcome into a repeatable operational standard.
How We Do It?
  • Agency mindset: we view the system as a whole, not through a single window.
  • Algorithmic thinking: grows with speed, accuracy, and data.
  • We use power to protect the good and leave no room for the bad.
“We hold the scale; competence weighs, results determine balance.”

Not show, but impact. Not process, but result. Not aesthetics, but superiority.

Mission

Operation Shaping Culture

Redefining trends by determining the future direction, triggering cultural transformations, and creating global impact by moving the values we cultivate to the desired position according to our strategic vision.
